About

Mexican‑Australian. Sydney‑sider. Curious by nature.

Mauricio is a Mexican-Australian based in Sydney who enjoys photography but hasn't quite gotten to the point of calling himself a professional, and that's perfectly fine with him.

He likes keeping memories of daily moments in life, whether it's during a commute to work or out exploring on a holiday. He's also an aviation enthusiast, always looking up when something flies overhead.

This space was created to stay away from social media likes, while still having a way to share moments through his lens, whether from a mobile phone or a DSLR camera.
